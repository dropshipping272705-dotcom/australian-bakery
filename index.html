Our Story

Australian Bakery & Cafe

Nestled in the heart of Civil Lines, Gurdaspur, Australian Bakery & Cafe has been crafting memorable flavours since 2013. What began as a passion for artisan baking has grown into the city's most beloved destination for exquisite cakes, Italian-inspired delicacies, and freshly brewed coffee.

Rated 4.4 ★ by over 546 delighted patrons, we take pride in our creative cake designs, warm hospitality, and an inviting ambience that keeps guests coming back. From vegan-friendly treats to hearty sandwiches and quick bites — there's something for every palate.
